&quot;People Are Wicked&quot;: Air Peace Website Cloned By Fraudsters, Nigerians React, Give Solutions

 Posted on August 19, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  415 words  |  Billy Koelling
  • Scammers have built a fake website meant to defraud people booking Air Peace's Lagos to London direct flight
  • Air Peace started flying the lucrative Lagos-London route on March 30, attracting widespread applause from air passengers
  • Nigerians are reacting in different ways to the reported cloning of the Air Peace website, with one X user saying it was a wicked act by fraudsters

An X user has described fraudsters who built a fake version of the Air Peace website as wicked.

This is coming after Air Peace started flying the Lagos-London route, and many Nigerians applauded the airline.

A post on X by Air Peace confirmed that there was a fake website impersonating the airline and warned air passengers to beware.

Air Peace disclosed the name of the website flyairpeace.uk and gave its authentic website, flyairpeace.com.

Air Peace begins sale of ticket

Meanwhile, Air Peace kicked off the sale of its flight schedules for its London service starting March 30, 2024.

The airline said the tickets for the London route are now available on the website at unbeatable rates.

It also disclosed that there is a special discount for Nigerian students who choose to travel to the UK using the airline.
