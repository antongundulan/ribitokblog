Anne Hathaway's Latest Cozy Winter Outfit Is So Easy to Replicate

 Posted on August 15, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  315 words  |  Patria Henriques

Whether she’s on the screen, on a film festival red carpet, or running errands around town, Anne Hathaway never fails to inspire us with her style.

Most recently, the actor was spotted on a day out in New York City, after making an appearance on The Tonight Show Starring Jimmy Fallon, braving the chilly weather in a casually sophisticated look that we’re already dying to replicate. The outfit included a cozy knit cardigan in a bright lavender hue, by Lisa Yang, with pretty, ribbed details at the sleeves and pockets, a deep V neck, and large tortoiseshell buttons. (Similar styles are available at Sézane, Ssense, and Cabana Canary.)

Raymond Hall//Getty Images

Hathaway layered the sweater over a classic long-sleeved white tee by Leset and paired it with the most perfect white wide-leg pants by DL1961, once again flouting the “no white after Labor Day” mandate. (Shop similar styles at Favorite Daughter, Altuzarra, Kotn, and Mango.)

Raymond Hall//Getty Images

Hathaway mixed luxury with comfort with a pair of chunky white and tan sneakers (shop the look at Coach and Tory Burch), and carried a black Hermès Birkin bag (one is currently on sale at Vestiaire Collective) with an adorable Hermès pegasus horse charm hanging from the strap.

In a peak “quiet luxury” moment, the Devil Wears Prada star accessorized the ensemble simply with elongated black sunglasses (shop a similar style at Prada) and small gold hoop earrings. She wore her hair down, straight, and had on rosy, natural makeup, including a glossy raspberry lip.
