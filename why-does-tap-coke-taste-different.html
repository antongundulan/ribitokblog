Why does my canned Coke taste weird?

Once cans are assembled, the inside of each gets coated with a water-based polymer. The polymer coating keeps the soda and aluminum molecules separate, protecting the can from corrosion. Without that barrier, the aluminum could corrode (rust) and give the soda an intense metallic taste.
