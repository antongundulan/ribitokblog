Zak Bagans Of Haunted Museum Bio, Married, Wife, Daughter, Girlfriend

In the world today there are those who believe there is something more than the physical world; the paranormal. But not everyone believes that, and there are a lot of skeptics. From time to time, skeptics convert to believers, and that’s exactly what happened to Zak Bagans, host of the Travel Channel series Deadly Possessions , which also houses his Las Vegas Haunted Museum. What could have made Zak a believer? We’ll find out below.

Zak Bagan’s bio

Zak is a paranormal investigator born on the 5th of April 1977 in Washington, D.C. He is the son of Nancy Knapp, an interior designer; and brother of Meredith Bagans. However, the identity of his father is unknown. He attended Glenbard West High School in Glen Ellyn, Illinois and graduated in 1995. He then attended and graduated from the Motion Picture Institute in Michigan. After graduating, he moved to Las Vegas with ambitions to make documentaries, but before that he was a wedding disc jockey.

Zak Bagans believed in the paranormal when he encountered the spirit of a suicidal woman in his former home in Trenton, Michigan. From there he shifted his focus to paranormal investigations.

career

2004 was the year Zak began his paranormal investigations. He teamed up with Nick Groff and Aaron Goodwin and began producing the Ghost Adventures series . The series followed Zak and his team as they explored locations that were reportedly haunted. In the first episode, the team stayed at the supposedly haunted nightclub Bobby Mackey’s Music World. Bagans claimed that he had mysterious claw marks on his back while he spent the night there.

While the series aired, he came under fire from paranormal enthusiasts for his aggression and confrontation when dealing with ghosts. Zak defended his methods by saying that he only used aggression against spirits that harmed the living. The series ran for 14 seasons, ending its final season in May 2017.

2011 had him as the creator and host of the spin-off Paranormal Challenge , a series with a competitive edge. Paranormal Challenge pitted ghost hunters from across the US against each other as they gathered evidence by spending nights in supposedly haunted locations across the US. The evidence gathered by the teams was judged by a panel of paranormal experts to determine the winner. The series ran for a season of 12 episodes.

That same year, Zak Bagans had narrated and hosts the Travel Channel special Ultimate Travel: Legends of the Park . The show was about America’s national parks and how they were haunted.

His career as a paranormal investigator inspired him to become an author. He co-wrote Dark World: Into the Shadows with Ghost Adventures Crew’s Lead Investigator starring Kelly Crigger. It debuted at no. 18 on the New York Times Best Seller list on the 23rd of September 2011. He has since successfully published three more titles: Darkness Optional: The Holy Truth (2013), I Am Haunted (2014) and I Am Haunted: Living Through the Dead (2015).

Married / wife or girlfriend, daughter

Many TV personalities tend to keep details of their personal affairs as minimal as possible. Zak Bagans is no different. Despite his popularity, he lives a low-key life, leaving fans wondering if he’s in any relationship or not.

Back in 2014, Zak shared a photo on Twitter announcing an engagement and tweeted a photo of his fiancee Ashley. No further details were given after the tweet and many have concluded that the announcement was a joke since he made it on the 1st of April, April Fool ‘s Day.

Zak dated Christine Dolce for a while, but the relationship ended before her passing in 2017. There were rumors of a relationship with Marcy Delatorre, but she ended up being a fictional character created by fans.

There is currently no sign that he is in a relationship or married and Zak has denied rumors that he has a daughter.

wealth and wealth

Zak Bagans has had an impressive television career so far. He is a best-selling author, owns his own clothing line, Reaper, has a haunted house in Indiana and the Haunted Museum in Las Vegas. Combined, these things add up to a net worth of $5 million for the man, a truly impressive number.

altitude and other information

Zak’s full name is Zachary Alexander Bagans. He is a tall man, 183 cm tall and weighs 80 kg. He has an athletic body with brown hair and blue eyes. He is also a fan of tattoos and has at least seven on his body. These include a Celtic cross on his left arm and an angel/demon behind his neck.
