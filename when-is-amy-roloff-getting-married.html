When is Amy Roloff getting married? Little People Big World stars wedding explored Wed 4 Aug

 Posted on August 10, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  562 words  |  Billy Koelling

Little People Big World’s season 22 aired episode 13 titled About the Bride yesterday on August 3. The episode saw the Roloff family’s matriarch Amy trying on bridal dresses as her wedding with fiancé Chris Marek is just weeks away.

In a clip from the episode, the TLC star says: “Picking out a wedding dress is awfully daunting. Every bride, whether young or older, wants to feel good in a dress, wants to be the star of the event, of course.”

Let’s check out Amy Roloff‘s upcoming wedding.

When is Amy Roloff getting married?

Amy Roloff took to her Instagram on Aug.1 and posted a picture with her fiancé. In the caption, she announced that their wedding was only “28 days away” at the time.

View Instagram Post

Moreover, in the June 29 episode of the show, Amy says, “Chris and I have made the final decision on the wedding venue and we’re getting married at Roloff Farms.” On the show, Chris had previously stated that his dream location for their wedding would be the same spot where Amy’s daughter Molly tied the knot. This location is Roloff Farms.

At the moment, neither TLC nor the couple has revealed if their nuptials will be televised for Little People Big World‘s season 22. However, given the fact that the duo has shared their relationship and wedding preparations very openly so far, it can be assumed that the wedding would be televised.

How did Amy meet fiance Chris Marek?

Amy Roloff and Chris Marek have been a couple since 2016. The pair met at a singles mixer Paint and Sip event which Amy reluctantly attended. The TLC star has revealed that she was not ready to date at the time as she was still recovering from her divorce from Matt Roloff her husband of 27 years.

Amy has revealed on the show that she and Chris eventually got to talking. He reportedly asked her if she wanted a ride on his motorcycle, she said yes and the rest is history.

After a couple of years of dating, 59-year-old Chris who is a real estate agent by profession popped the big question in 2019 and of course, Amy (56) said yes. Their engagement was featured in a 2020 episode of the show.

‘Little People Big World’ stars’ Matt and Amy’s divorce explored

Amy and Matt Roloff got married in 1987 and remained together till 2016. The couple shares four children together, including twins Jeremy and Zach, 29, daughter Molly, 25, and Jacob, 22.

Both Amy and Matt have rarely opened up about their divorce on the show. However, in her 2019 book titled A Little Me, Amy alleged that Matt was involved with his current girlfriend Caryn Chandler while the two were still married.

Before Matt and Caryn began dating, the latter was a long-time employee of Roloff Farms. Matt responded to the cheating allegations in 2020 when he appeared on a podcast. The TLC star said, that the memoir wasn’t fair to him and that Amy had left many parts about their marriage troubles out of the book.
