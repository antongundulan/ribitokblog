Who is Ximena Morales Cuellar?

Ximena Morales Cuellar is a 24-year-old cast member on 90 Day Fiancé: Before The 90 Days season 5.

She comes from Colombia and meets her New Yorker boyfriend, Mike Berkowitz, for the first time in person during the TLC show.

Mike and Ximena met online and he flies out to Colombia to spend time with her on 90 Day Fiancé.

Ximena is a single mother to two young boys and she previously worked as a manicurist prior to the pandemic.

90 Day Fiancé: What is Ximena’s secret?

There have been many secrets unveiled between 90 Day Fiancé couples over the seasons. So, Ximena revealing that she has a secret is no huge surprise.

However, viewers of the show are obviously keen to find out what Ximena is going to tell Mike during episode 5.

Speaking to her friend during a snippet of the show, Ximena revealed that she can no longer have children, which may upset Mike.

Ximena said: “I suffered a lot with my two deliveries because to have a child by Caesarean is the worst pain other could go through.”

She added that she decided to have her tubes tied so that she definitely couldn’t have more children.

Does Mike want children?

Yes, Ximena said during season 5 episode 5 that she knows Mike wants to have children but she can’t have kids.

Speaking on 90 Day Fiancé, Mike said that he was looking for someone to settle down with, so it’s unlikely that he’s going to be expecting this news from Ximena.

Ximena added in a preview of episode 5 that she “knew it was a mistake not to tell him earlier” but she wanted him to go to Colombia so that they could see each other first and so that she could tell him in person.
