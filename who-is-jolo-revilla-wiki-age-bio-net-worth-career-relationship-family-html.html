  • Jolo Revilla, born on March 15, 1988, is a well-known Filipino Comedian, Actor, and Politician hailing from Quezon City, Philippines. He is actively associated with political parties such as United Nationalist Alliance, Lakas-CMD, and PDP-Laban.
  • In October 2010, Jolo participated in the Barangay elections and emerged as the President of the Association of Barangay Captains. He further contested and won the Vice-Governor elections of the Philippines in 2013, becoming the youngest governor at that time.
