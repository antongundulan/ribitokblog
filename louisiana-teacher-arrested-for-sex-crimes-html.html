A shocking video featuring a former Louisiana school teacher has sent shockwaves across the internet. In the video, the teacher is seen making inappropriate comments to an individual he claims to be tutoring. This distressing incident has led to the arrest of the teacher, prompting questions about the safety of students. Read on to uncover the full story surrounding this unsettling event.

The Arrest: Who is Jacob de la Paz?

The Louisiana teacher in question has been identified as Jacob de la Paz. He was apprehended on May 30, 2023, facing serious charges from the US Department of Homeland Security. Jacob de la Paz is accused of enticing a minor to create explicit material, a grave offense. The viral video that captured de la Paz’s explicit remarks further complicates the situation.

A Troubling History: Prior Incidents Surface

Jacob de la Paz, aged 33, was employed as a mathematics teacher at St. Thomas More Catholic High School in Lafayette, Louisiana. Before joining this institution, he taught at Vermilion Parish School for a period of five years, spanning from 2017 to 202Notably, this is not the first instance of de la Paz’s involvement in inappropriate conduct with a student. He previously faced disciplinary action in Arkansas for sending an inappropriate message to a student.

Current Status: Detainment and School Response

Presently, Jacob de la Paz is detained in St. Martin Parish, Louisiana. It remains unclear whether he has sought legal counsel. The Rev. Michael J. Russo, Chancellor of St. Thomas More, addressed concerned parents, confirming the termination of de la Paz’s employment. In his message, Rev. Russo stated, “School officials have no reports, evidence, or reason to believe that Mr. De La Paz engaged in any type of inappropriate behavior or misconduct with any students at the school.” He also emphasized de la Paz’s effectiveness as a teacher, noting an absence of prior complaints.

Conclusion: An Ongoing Investigation

As the investigation unfolds, authorities are committed to ensuring the safety and well-being of students.
