AEW Revolution 2023: Full Match Card and Start Time

 Posted on August 30, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  335 words  |  Patria Henriques

AEW will be presenting their first major pay-per-view of the year with Revolution. Naturally, fans are quite excited about the upcoming event for a multitude of reasons. The show’s outcome will have several ramifications for the upcoming events as well.

Fans are ready for the highly-anticipated third annual Revolution event presented by AEW, broadcasting live from the Chase Center in San Francisco, California on Sunday, March 5. The event is set to kick off at 8pm ET with the pre-show beginning at 7 p.m. ET.

It features a stacked card headlined by a 60-minute Iron Man match between AEW Champion MJF and Bryan Danielson. The event will also showcase a thrilling battle for the AEW Women’s Championship, as Jamie Hayter defends against former WWE Superstars Ruby Soho and Saraya, better known as Paige.

The intense feud between Hangman Adam Page and Jon Moxley will culminate in a Texas Deathmatch, sure to be a brutal and unforgettable encounter. Meanwhile, Ricky Starks and Chris Jericho will finally settle their score in a high-stakes match, with the JAS banned from ringside.

MJF vs. Daniel Bryan Danielson in a 60-minute Ironman match for the AEW World Championship

Jon Moxley vs. Hangman Page in a Texas Deathmatch
The Elite Vs. The HOB for the AEW Trios Championships

Jamie Hayter vs. Saraya Vs. Ruby Soho in a triple-threat match for the AEW Women’s Championship

Jungle Boy vs. Christian Cage

Chris Jericho vs. Ricky Starks

Samoa Joe vs. Wardlow for AEW TNT Championship

The Gunns vs. The Acclaimed Vs. Jay Lethal Vs. Orange Cassidy & Danhausen for the AEW Tag Team Championships
