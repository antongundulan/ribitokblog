Las Vegas Rain: Prepare for a Puddle Party (Maybe)

Las Vegas, the city of bright lights, clinking slot machines, and...uh...rain? That's right, folks, we're here to discuss the burning question (or perhaps the question that should never be asked in this desert): how much rain is Sin City supposed to get?

Rain in Vegas? Is This a Mirage?

Let's face it, rain in Las Vegas is about as common as finding a buffet that doesn't require a mortgage payment to enter. But hey, even the driest places can get a sprinkle sometimes, right? According to the latest forecast, Las Vegas is currently basking in glorious sunshine with a temperature that could fry an egg on the sidewalk (don't try this at home, folks).

However, there's always a chance things could change. Weather forecasts can be as fickle as a slot machine payout, so stay tuned!

But Seriously, Folks...

While the rain gods might decide to bless us with a surprise shower, it's probably best not to pack your raincoat just yet. Las Vegas averages about four inches of rain per year, which is basically a spittoon compared to most places.

So, what can you expect? Maybe a brief downpour that will cause the tourists to whip out their phones and capture this once-in-a-lifetime event. Perhaps a sprinkle that will leave the desert floor slightly less dusty...for a whole five minutes.

Here's the bottom line: rain in Vegas is a novelty, not a necessity.

Vegas Rain FAQ: You Asked, We (Maybe) Answered

How to prepare for rain in Vegas?

  • Don't bother. You're more likely to win the jackpot than get caught in a downpour.

How to survive a rain shower in Vegas?

  • Run for cover under the nearest awning. It won't last long.

How to gamble on the weather?

  • We don't recommend it. Unless you have a superpower that predicts the weather, stick to the roulette wheel.

How to tell if it's raining or if you just spilled your margarita?

  • Taste test. Rain is not salty (usually).

How much fun can you have in a little bit of rain?

  • A Vegas-sized amount! Sing and dance in the puddles, just watch out for rogue slot machine tokens.
