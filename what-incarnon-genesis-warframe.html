What is Incarnon Genesis in Warframe?

 Posted on August 16, 2024  |  4 minutes  |  827 words  |  Billy Koelling

The latest update in Warframe, The Duviri Paradox, spices up the Drifter gameplay with new melee mechanics and a whole swath of fresh open-world activities. Duviri is the first free-roam map to come out after the Cambion Drift from the Heart of Deimos update in 2020. Duvuri Paradox was delayed almost half a year from its original launch window, winter 2022.

The mixed reception of Lua's Prey, the interim update, and the postponement is arguably recouped with all the extra potential for content that Duviri Paradox presents. While most of it focuses on the independent Drifter gameplay, the Incarnan Genesis system is a great platform to make outdated weapons great again.

How to get Incarnon Genesis for your weapon in Warframe?

Warframe boasts a massive arsenal of some 400 weapons, split between Primary, Secondary, and Melee slots. This is without even accounting for the customizable weapons with modular parts like kit guns. Incarnon Weapons are the latest inclusion to bring more flavor to the collection.

Added in the Angels of the Zariman update, Incarnon weapons are procurable specifically from Cavalero in Chrysalith. These five Incarnon weapons stand out by having unique Incarnon forms, an enhanced version triggered by doing particular things.

These weapons also have upgradable perk points across five Evolution tiers. With different perks, the weapon's behavior branches out into its own divergent upgrade paths. Warframe has always had the potential for vastly different builds on the same weapon, but this brings it to the next level.

Simply put, the Incarnon Genesis is the same system applied on top of older Warframe weapons. The way you attain these forms is by applying Incarnon Adapters to these weapons once available.

These adapters are obtainable only through the Steel Path Circuit mode. Thus, to obtain them, you need to have the following:

  • You must unlock the Steel Path mode by completing all the nodes on the Star Chart.
  • You must have access to The Circuit, obtainable by finishing the new Duviri Paradox quest.
  • You must have access to Chrysalith via completion of Angels of The Zariman.

Once you have all three, you can access the Duviri Spiral and Choose the Circuit. While the normal Circuit mode grants Warframe blueprints and components, the Steel Path variant provides Incarnon Geneses for particular weapons.

These weapons change weekly, and you must select two out of the five each week to get your own Circuit reward path. Note that the first weapon you pick will be on the fifth reward tier and the second on the tenth. Afterward, it is a matter of grinding it out in the Steel Path Circuit before the next week.

Once an Incarnon Genesis adapter is obtained, it can be activated through Calavero, a vendor in the hub area of the Zariman Ten Zero.
