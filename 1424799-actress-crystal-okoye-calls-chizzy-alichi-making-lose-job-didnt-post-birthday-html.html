In the video posted on her page, Okoye alleged that Alichi had her kicked off a movie set after she discovered they were both working on the job together.

Okoye explained that Alichi reacted that way because she did not post her on social media on her birthday.

Giving insight into the gist, Okoye narrated how she had travelled all the way from Asaba to Ebonyi for Alichi's wedding, even going ahead to celebrate her birthday on social media.
