See Kylie Jenner As A Sexy Bride Of Frankenstein

 Posted on August 14, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  537 words  |  Tandra Barner

Kylie Jenner posted a picture on Instagram of her dressed up as the sexy bride of Frankenstein for Halloween

By Mark McKee | Published 1 year ago

Kylie Jenner is joining the ranks of great celebrity Halloween costume wearers. Dwayne Johnson as the Incredible Hulk, Kevin Hart as Dwayne Johnson, Harry Styles as Rocketman Elton John, and of course, nobody will ever forget Kim Kardashian’s Catwoman; Halloween is a time when everyone gets to dress up as their favorite characters on screen, in real life, or just out of their imaginations.

However, even those that get to dress up and play other people every day like to have fun on Halloween night, and Kim Kardashian’s sister is the one making a splash during the spooky season of 2022. Kylie Jenner took to Instagram to show off her costume as the Bride of Frankenstein, and everyone is looking. 

There are three posts of the 25-year-old mogul, Kylie Jenner, rocking the Bride of Frankenstein costume, with the social media reveal featuring her on an upright examination table; the images, of course, are black and white in an effort to harken back to the original monster films when she was created.

While the gown may be one of the last things anyone notices in the incredibly well-done photo, it is one of the more essential features as it is a custom dress by Jean Paul Gaultier. This wasn’t simply a look for a Halloween party, this was a production as the stage is set to look like the original movie scene, and her hair and makeup were made to look like the on-screen monster, all the way down to the classic and distinct white streaks in her long locks.

This wasn’t the only post that features her look, this kind of time and dedication to a movie-level costume deserves attention, and Kylie Jenner sought it with three more posts that garnered a running total of 12 million likes and 20 thousand comments.

The second look after the incredible premiere was a two-photo collection that focused more on the makeup and hair than the gown, as the custom gown was hidden under a more draped white dress. The third set of photos featured Jenner in a ripped top, with no bottoms, and a knife, and a close-up of half of her face, giving the best look at her makeup and white streaks in her hair. 

The fourth post featured the same bottomless look as the last, but the billionaire switched to model mode, striking poses on the floor, and the most recent post is a reel of her striking a pose and embodying the character.

Of course, Kylie Jenner wouldn’t forget to tag the rest of the team; while the dress was a custom job by Jean Paul Gaultier, she also tagged the rest of the team responsible for the look. Including hairstylist Tokyo Stylez, best known for her appearances on E! ’s Keeping up with the Kardashians and Life of Kylie as Kylie Jenner’s personal hairstylist, and her makeup artist Ariel Tejada.

While most celebrities go all out for their Halloween costumes, Kylie herself has been seen as characters like The Little Mermaid, a Power Ranger, a Playboy bunny, and Christina Aguilera; her newest look puts all the rest of them to shame.
