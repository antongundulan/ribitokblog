Pokemon quotes are loved by the franchise's fans. They are a nostalgic reminder to all the 90s kids who grew up with the anime.

When it comes to Pokemon Go, the tagline, 'Gotta catch 'em all,' is the most popular one. In the anime and movies, the most known Pokemon catch phrases are 'Gotta catch 'em all!' and 'I choose you'.

These two Pokemon phrases have long been associated with the game and trainers. Fans of the game, film and anime would be interested to know that when the original anime was made, it had a different slogan all together.

Nintendo had intended that the slogan would be 'Catch 'em if you can.'
