Michelle began her career with the BBC in 2005 as a program maker. Over the years, she has made significant contributions to various shows, including co-hosting the CBBC series “All Over the Place” from 2013 and joining the BBC’s “Crimewatch Roadshow” as a correspondent in 2015.
