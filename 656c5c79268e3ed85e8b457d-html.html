Lil Wayne on why he thinks Taylor Swift won't support Travis Kelce in Chiefs vs Packers game

 Posted on August 28, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  303 words  |  Kelle Repass

Taylor Swift's appearance at the Kansas City Chiefs' game versus the Green Bay Packers is still unconfirmed as fans wonder whether she will turn out to watch her boyfriend, Travis Kelce, but Lil Wayne believes she won't as it's too cold.

Swift has just completed her Eras Tour leg in Argentina and Brazil in the southern hemisphere where it is currently the summer, so hot that a fan lost their life due to the heat.

But in Green Bay, Wisconsin, it's currently freezing temperature or significantly below with temperatures dropping to 23 Fahrenheit leaving the "Lollipop" singer believing that Swift will skip on Week 13 of the NFL season.

"Packers win over Chiefs," Lil Wayne said to "Undisputed". "They get them 31-17... It's too cold out there for Taylor Swift, even she isn't coming."

Packers vs Chiefs forecast

The match is currently scheduled to take place on Sunday, December 3 at 20:20 ET when the night has drawn in and the cold begins to set, making it a game where both players will be running hard just to keep warm.

The Chiefs currently lead the AFC West with an 8-3 record, two games ahead of a resurgent Denver Broncos outfit that have won five on the spin. They head into the Wisconsin match-up having defeated the Las Vegas Raiders 31-17 just after being handed a loss by the Philadelphia Eagles.

The Packers, on the other hand, are third in the NFC North with a losing record (.455), with their most recent turn out being a 29-22 surprise victory over the divisional leaders, the Detroit Lions. The Packers can improve to a .500 record by getting their sixth win of the season against the Chiefs, giving them a late chance at a postseason push.
