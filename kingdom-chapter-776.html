Kingdom Chapter 776:- Kingdom is a popular manga series written and illustrated by Yasuhisa Hara. The story follows the adventures of Shin, a young orphan who dreams of becoming a great general in ancient China during the Warring States period. Kingdom has been serialized in Weekly Young Jump magazine since 2006 and has over 60 million copies in circulation.

The manga is currently in the Western Zhao Invasion arc, where Qin is trying to conquer the state of Zhao and face the formidable general Riboku. The latest chapter, 775, ended with a cliffhanger as Shin and his Hi Shin Unit were about to clash with Riboku and his elite soldiers.

Fans are eagerly waiting for the next chapter, 776, to see the outcome of this epic battle. Here is everything you need to know about Kingdom Chapter 776, including the release date, time zone, countdown, spoilers, raw scan, recap, and where to read.

Recap of Previous Chapter 775

In the previous chapter, we saw the continuation of the battle between Qin and Zhao. Shin and his Hi Shin Unit were advancing towards Riboku’s headquarters, while Kyoukai and her unit were fighting against Futei and his men. Kyoukai managed to injure Futei and forced him to retreat, while Shin was getting closer to Riboku.

Meanwhile, Ousen and his army were facing the Zhao center army led by Shun Sui Ju. Ousen ordered his men to form a defensive formation and wait for the right moment to attack. He also sent a messenger to Akou, who was leading the right wing, to inform him of the situation.

On the other hand, Riboku was confident that he could stop Shin and his unit. He ordered his men to prepare for the final confrontation and said that he would personally deal with Shin. He also revealed that he had a secret weapon that he had been saving for this occasion. He said that he had a special arrow that could pierce through any armor and kill any general.

The chapter ended with Shin and Riboku facing each other on the battlefield, ready to clash.
