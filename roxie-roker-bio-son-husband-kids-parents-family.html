Roxie Roker bio

Roxie Roker was born Roxie Albertha Roker on August 28, 1929. After graduating, she returned to New York to pursue a career in acting. Well, fate initially had a different plan. First, she got a job in the NBC office in New York. While working for NBC, she would take every opportunity to work on stage. She worked for a while with the Negro Ensemble company, then began her professional acting career. Roxie has starred in plays like Les Noirs, Ododo, Rosalee among others.

She then worked as a television anchor at WNEW-TV in New York, where she hosted a show that explored events in Brooklyn called Inside Bedford-Stuyvesant. Roxie Roker continued acting and soon began starring in movies and TV shows. Today, she is best known for her role in the television series The Jefferson.
